AUTHORS

We accept proposals from writers, artists, and editors

OUR TITLES

Grid Books publishes a variety of perspectives expressed
in creative and collaborative formats, including poetry,
fine art, and scholarly editions, and projects
encompassing oral history.

OUR HISTORY

In 2003, Somerville poets Tam Lin Neville and Bert Stern
began the press to provide a forum for poets who are
sometimes overlooked by the current marketplace.

OFF THE GRID PRESS
